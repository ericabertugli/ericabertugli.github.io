Privacy Policy

This website respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with the website. Analytics data is only collected if you accept cookies via the consent banner.

When you accept cookies, Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Your approximate location (country/city level)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Referral source (how you found this site)

This data is used in aggregated form to understand usage and improve the website. It is sent to and processed by Google Analytics (a Google service) as a third-party analytics provider and may include identifiers such as your IP address and device identifiers. I do not use Google Analytics to collect information like your name or email address, and I do not sell your analytics data. For more details on how Google handles this information, see the Google Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This site uses the following cookies:

  • cookie_consent (localStorage): Stores your cookie preference (accept/decline). This is essential for remembering your choice.
  • Google Analytics cookies: Only set if you accept analytics. These include _ga and _ga_* cookies used to distinguish users and sessions.

Your Choices

You can decline cookies when prompted by the banner. If you decline, no analytics data will be collected. You can also clear your browser's localStorage to reset your preference.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google may collect your IP address.
  • OpenStreetMap / CARTO: For map tiles on the skating and travel pages.
  • Wikiloc: For embedded route maps on the skating page.

Contact

For privacy concerns, you can reach me via the contact links on the homepage.
